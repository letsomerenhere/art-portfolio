Jacob Khalil is a New York based artist, formally educated in Fashion Design (Pratt Institute, BFA).

His work is grounded in historical artistic references and a production aesthetic that often recontextualizes classic pieces.

With a strong background in classical painting and art history, he respects and honors craftsmanship through his own deliberate attention to detail.

His work is inspired heavily by music and poetry, with focus on existential themes.
